Andy Peters

Principle Investigator

CV · Github
Andy grew up outside of Philadelphia and bounced around the US before coming to the UK. He got his PhD in Takaki Komiyama's lab at UC San Diego studying motor cortex activity over learning, then moved to the Matteo Carandini & Kenneth Harris' lab at University College London for a postdoc to study interactions between the cortex and striatum. In his free time he likes playing drums (less often) and mandolin (more often but worse).
